Stopwatch User Guide

Welcome to the Stopwatch Web App User Guide. Here are the basic instructions on how to use the stopwatch:

  1. Start the stopwatch by clicking the "Start" button.
  2. Pause the stopwatch at any time by clicking the "stop" button.
  3. Resume the stopwatch by clicking the "Resume" button.
  4. Reset the stopwatch to zero by clicking the "Reset" button.
  5. save the stopwatch to zero by double clicking the "lap" button.
  6. You can also save a recorded time to your saved stopwatch list by hover on "SaveWatchList" button and select on the list.
  7. You can also see the all saved list on clicking on the Saved Stop Watch
  8. by clicking on the dark_toggle button on top coarner you can make window as dark style
